INRIA CHILE IS PLEASED TO PRESENT THE JOURNÉES SCIENTIFIQUE 2022

Welcome to the Journées Scientifiques Inria Chile 2022, a meeting 100% online to analyze and to know about advances in science and technology, in a French-Chilean encounter promoted by Inria Chile. In its first version, we will address these main topics: artificial intelligence, internet of things, applied mathematics, oceans, climate, biodiversity, astronomy and open science. Be part of the conversation about the link between science and industry and how to solve complex problems that simplify processes and improve productivity.

We will present models of technological and scientific development, with teams that gather Chilean and French researchers and engineers, working on great innovative ideas. Together we want to move forward with new scientific and technological solutions to contribute to our society and our economy. Inria Chile and the Journées Scientifiques 2022 invite you to participate and rethink our reality.
